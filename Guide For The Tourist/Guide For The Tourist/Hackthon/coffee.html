HELP ME OUT IN HYDERABAD





Best Cafe's in Hyderabad

Click on the image for location!

1. Coffee Cup - Beautiful Brewed Coffees and Breakfast Platters:

Located a little far from the city; this place is known for its beautifully brewed coffees and breakfast platters. Its decor, neither too extravagant nor shabbily done, is an absolute win. Live music and a lovely ambience together complement the food here which is delectable. They even have a collection of board and card games for you to kill time with as the service can be a tad slow. Visit during karaoke nights for an experience you will remember. Reservations are recommended.

Must-try dishes: Chicken Penne Pasta, Fish n Chips, Chocolate Brownie Cold Coffee, Brownie
Location: Above Canara Bank, Sainikpuri, Secunderabad
Cost for two: INR 800
Timings: 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM


2. The Roastery Coffee House - a Bungalow-turned Cafe to Chill at:

Bungalow-turned-Cafe, this is a cosy little establishment tucked away from the busier roads of the city. The ambience is very home-like and spells out tranquillity through its plain white walls. And if you like to dine in the lap of nature, a well-maintained garden awaits you in the backyard. The menu offers a plethora of options apart from the coffee which is roasted and brewed in-house. Coffees from the 'Artisan Blends', 'Pour-Over' and 'Cold Brew' sections especially are a must try. Drop in by 10 AM for a scrumptious breakfast.

Must try dishes: Mac 'n' Cheese, Onion Rings
Location: House 418, Road 14, Banjara Hills
Cost for two: INR 700
Timings: 9:30 AM to 11:00 PM


3. Autumn Leaf Cafe - Sip a Cup of Coffee amidst a Quite Courtyard :

A cafe that doesn't feel like a cafe - true to its name, you will get the feeling of being seated in an area filled with autumn greenery. A quiet courtyard of a bungalow, if you will. With distressed, mismatched furniture and artefacts adorning every nook and cranny of this place, it almost gives out Bohemian vibes. Their brunch menu is diverse. You may opt for indoor seating as well if the weather gods are too harsh. Impressively, they have a neat little solution for that - mist spraying table fans.

Must try dishes: English breakfast, Mocktails, Pasta
Location: Road 41, Jubilee Hills
Cost for two: INR 1000
Timings: Mon-Thu 12:00 PM to 10:30 PM, Fri 8 PM to 10:30 PM, Sat-Sun 9 AM to 10:30 PM


4. The Gallery Cafe - Treat your Eyes as you Treat your Taste Buds:

Sit back, relax and have a good time at this quaint little art gallery-cum-cafe if you are looking for a casual yet soothing vibe. The decor is minimalistic, and the art pieces speak for themselves against the serene white walls. They even provide stationery to diners to create their masterpieces. And here's some respite for vegetarians in the meat-loving city of Hyderabad: this place serves pure vegetarian food.

Must try dishes: Pasta, Sandwiches, Chocolate Shake, Waffles
Location: Road 10, Banjara Hills
Cost for two: INR 800
Timings: 11:30 AM to 10:30 PM


5. Driven Cafe - Dine with all that Road-Tripping stuff around:

All you bikers and vintage car enthusiasts listen up for this one - cause you will love dining here with all the road-tripping stuff around - bike accessories, pictures and actual vehicles. They even allow you to rent those super cool bikes! The interiors are so rustic yet extraordinarily plush and classy that you get the feel of dining in some foreign country. What's even better is that it remains open until late at night. Midnight cravings sorted!

Must try dishes: Hot Chocolate, Cold Coffee, Donut, Sandwiches
Location: Kapil Towers, Opposite ICICI Bank, ISB Road, Financial District, Nanakramguda, Gachibowli
Cost for two: INR 600
Timings: 12:00 AM to 4:00 AM


6. Beyond Coffee - Another treat for an Art Lover:

This is another Jubilee Hills marvel worth checking out. And if you happen to be an art lover, you are in luck because this place also doubles as an art gallery with some brilliantly painted pictures, giving a sharp and pleasant contrast to its otherwise plain interiors. Vibes of peace settle you in here and isn't that the perfect backdrop for a chill conversation with friends? They even have outdoor seating for sheesha lovers.

Must try dishes: English breakfast, Coffee, Pasta
Location: Road 36, Jubilee Hills
Cost for two: INR 850
Timings: 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM


7. Karachi Cafe - a Vintage Decor that will surely give you a Wave of Nostalgia:

A trendy Parsi cafe with vintage decor, this one serves the best Irani Chai. You are sure to get hit by a wave of nostalgia when you look around this well-thought-of, aesthetic little cafe. Wooden furniture, colourful props and pictures are the main highlights.

Must try dishes: Irani Chai, Bun Maska, Nutella Jar, Keema Pav
Location: Multiple Outlets - Gachibowli, Hitech City, Karkhana
Cost for two: INR 600
Timings: 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM


8. Chit Chat Chai - Cafes aren't just about Coffee are they?:

Tea fans, we've got you covered too. This spacious cafe offers excellent options for cutting chai and a warm atmosphere to complement that. Laden with books, carom board and a foosball table, it is apt for a casual hangout with buddies on the weekend - living up to its name entirely. Teas are natural and fresh, and they also have a few coffee options too. It has a pop shop with some offbeat, creative stuff like fridge magnets, paintings, etc. on sale.

Must try dishes: Masala Chai, Masala Maggi, Sandwiches, Bun Makkhan
Location: Road 37, Kavuri Hills Phase 1, CBI Colony, Jubilee Hills
Cost for two: INR 500
Timings: 10:00 AM to 12:00 AM

9. Cafe Graffiti - A Cafe Unlike its Name:

Tight on a budget yet don't want to compromise on taste - Head to Cafe Graffiti because it offers finger-licking-good food tailored to fit a college student's wallet. Contrary to its name, it has plain walls and a pretty straightforward ambience but packs in a lot of power in its kitchen.

Must try dishes: Chicken BBQ Burger, Sandwiches, Thick Shakes, Pasta Arrabiata, Potato Wedges
Location: Road 13, Banjara Hills
Cost for two: INR 500
Timings: 11:00 AM to 12:00 AM


10. Chai Kahani - Where Tea tells Stories:

Another tea lover's delight, this is a cute little spot in Kompally. The outdoor setup of this place is sombre and beautiful - perfect for that date you've been planning. It is located on the Medchal Highway and can be easily missed if you don't look for it. They have other outlets across the city.

Must try dishes: Cookie Monster Shake, Sandwiches, Tea, Waffles
Location: Medchal Highway, Kompally; Multiple outlets
Cost for two: INR 450
Timings: 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM
