Best places for Shopping in Hyderabad

Click on the image for location!

1. Laad Bazaar:

One of the most popular markets for street shopping in Hyderabad is the Laad Bazar. Popularly the oldest markets in Hyderabad, it was established at least 200 years ago and it’s known primarily for its huge collection of bangles earning it the name “Choori Bazaar”. Apart from bangles, you can find a number of items such as pearls, jewelry, semi-precious stones, silverware, Bidri ware, handicrafts, kalamkari, silk, perfumes and so much more! Since cars and rickshaws are barred in the area, you can walk through the busy lanes and admire the rich collection of items. Make sure you have good bargaining skills for the best place for shopping in Hyderabad.

Location: Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad
Timings: 11 AM to 10:30 PM
Shopping Type: Street shopping
Popular For: Bagles, jewelry, semi-precious stones, handicrafts, silk materials, and others.

2.Manjeera Mall:

This is a sprawling mall, covering an area of about 4,50,000 square feet and has 6 floors where you can shop your heart out. This mall is considered to be one of the ideal places for shopping in Hyderabad. Moreover, you can make the most of your visit by exploring the entertainment areas as well as restaurants in this mall. It is the best place for shopping in Hyderabad.

Location: JNTU Rd, Kukatpally Housing Board Colony, Kukatpally, Hyderabad, Telangana 500072
Timings: 10:30 am to 11:00 pm
Shopping Type: Mall
Popular For: clothing accessories, cafes, footwear

3. GVK One Mall:

The mall is established in the Banjara Hills region. This place is ideal for those looking for shopping places that are away from the bustling streets of Hyderabad and want to shop their heart out without any inconvenience. Since it’s a mall, you can have a fulfilling shopping experience across various popular brands available here.

Location: Rd Number 1, Balapur Basthi, Banjara Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana 500034
Timings: 11:00 am to 10:00 pm
Shopping Type: Mall
Popular For: clothes, accessories, food

4. Jummerat Bazaar:

Jummerat Bazaar, also known as Chor Bazaar, is known for being one of the oldest street markets in Hyderabad. This popular flea market serves as the main hub of buying ancient and unique items. With a large array of good old furniture, kitchen tools, home equipment & home décor items, the bazaar is undoubtedly one of the most visited markets and one of the many popular tourist places in Hyderabad. The best part about street shopping in Hyderabad, especially from this bazaar is that it offers the opportunity to buy everything at the most nominal prices.

Location: Near Begum Nagar
Timings: 9 AM onwards (every Thursday)
Shopping Type: Street shopping
Popular For: Home décor items, ancient furniture, home equipment, and others.

5.Shahran Market:

Ladies, if you wish to buy traditional clothes at the most nominal prices, Shahran Market is the best place for shopping in Hyderabad. The market is primarily popular for selling burqas apart from offering salwars and other women’s fashion styles. The market also boasts a large manufacturing unit inside the market which deals with the export of quality burqas. Tourists can even try the local haleem while shopping in the busy streets.

Location: Near Charminar
Timings: 5.00 AM – 12.00 PM (Thursday)
Shopping Type: Street Shopping
Popular For: Traditional clothing especially burqas.

6. Perfume Market:

Located between Moti Chawk and Lal Bazar, Perfume Market is a delightful place to visit and indulge in the strong senses. Comprising a varied row of shops selling attar along with locally produced perfumes, the market is a treat to visit. Tourists can enjoy perfumes packed in tiny glass vials. The best part about these perfumes is that each of them is created with a base of fragrances like musk, rose, jasmine and sandalwood oil. There are family-run shops of Hyderabad perfumers located near Charminar in Pathergatti for tourists to indulge in the best of the natural perfume collection. Perfumes are one the of most interesting things to buy in Hyderabad.

Location: Laad Bazaar, near Charminar
Timings: 10 AM – 9.30 PM
Shopping Type: Street Shopping
Popular For: Natural perfume

7. Hyderabad Central:

If you have been looking at shopping in Hyderabad from renowned brands, then Hyderabad Central is the place to visit. The mall is the perfect home to one of the renowned fashion brands of the country and is available for everyone. Men, women, and children can find their necessary items in the mall. Apart from clothes, you can also avail of essential accessories such as travel gear, perfumes, and accessories in Hyderabad Mall. During festivals, the mall offers exciting offers and discounts which are a steal.

Location: Gachibowli
Timings: 11 AM – 9.30 PM (all days)
Shopping Type: Mall
Popular For: High-end branded shopping


8. Char Minar Bazaar:

Although with the infiltration of modernism, the charm of Char Minar Bazaar could not be interrupted. Standing tall and boasting price in the southern city of the country, Char Minar Bazaar is one of the most popular shopping places in the city. Offering street shopping, the market is popular worldwide for the pearls, especially Basra Pearl that is known for its embellishment with silver and gold. Standing out for the rustic charm, the market sells a wide array of clothing such as sherwanis, saris, and Hyderabad Khada dupattas and so on. The Pearl is one of the best things to buy in Hyderabad.

Location: Charminar Road, Char Kaman, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad
Timings: 9.30 AM – 5 PM (all days)
Shopping Type: Street shopping
Popular For: Hyderabad pearls, affordable clothing options

9. Inorbit Mall:

If you have been looking forward to spending a great time on the weekend by relaxing and shopping, Inorbit Mall is the place to visit. Offering some of the best retail brands in the country, the mall is the perfect blend of luxury or fashion style. Tourists are sure to feel entertained in this place with a number of entertainment experiences such as a cinema hall, gaming zone and artificial football turf. If you have been planning to rejuvenate and indulge in some guilt-free shopping, then Inorbit Mall is a must-visit place.

Location: S No.64, Inorbit Mall Road, APIIC Software Layout, Mindspace, Madhapur
Timings: 11 AM – 9.30 PM (all days)
Shopping Type: High end branded shopping
Popular For: Popular brands

10. Koti Sultan Bazar:

If you have been on the lookout for grabbing the latest silver jewelry and fashionable clothes, Koti Sultan Bazaar is the perfect shopping companion you need. This market dates back to almost 200 years old and has been popular during the colonial times and was then popular as Residency Market. At present, the market is popular for selling a number of house products in showrooms, flea markets and street vendors. The street market is perfect for shoppers to indulge in shopping with the utmost content and without ripping the pocket. Silver is among the best things to buy in Hyderabad.

Location: Koti
Timings: 10 AM – 11 PM
Shopping Type: street shopping
Popular For: Silver jewelry and fashionable clothes
