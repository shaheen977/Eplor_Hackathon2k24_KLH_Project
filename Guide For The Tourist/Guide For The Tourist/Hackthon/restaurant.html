1. AB’s Absolute Barbecue – Snack On Some Grill:

This is one of the best places to eat in Hyderabad that serves the famous ‘wish grill’. It’s fun ambience and quick service makes it a jam-packed restaurant, so making prior reservations would be a prudent choice. It has two outlets in the city, equally good, so you can choose from the one nearest you. The diners particularly appraise the ambience of the restaurant that is suitable for friends and family alike.

Location: Gachibowli, Jubilee Hills
Cuisine: European, Mediterranean, North Indian
Must Try: Grilled Pineapple and Kebabs
Contact Number: 073373 36829
Cost For Two: INR 1,500/-
Google Rating: 4.4/5

Booking a table in the ‘happy hour’ enables you to get a discount on your favourite dishes.
